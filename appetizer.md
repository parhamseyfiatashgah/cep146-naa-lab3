\# Peanut Butter Cookies



\## Ingredients

\- 1/2 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, softened  

\- 1/2 cup (120 g) peanut butter (creamy or chunky)  

\- 1/2 cup (100 g) granulated sugar  

\- 1/2 cup (110 g) brown sugar (packed)  

\- 1 large egg  

\- 1 tsp vanilla extract  

\- 1 1/4 cups (155 g) all-purpose flour  

\- 1/2 tsp baking soda  

\- 1/4 tsp salt  



\## Instructions

1\. Preheat oven to \*\*350°F (175°C)\*\*. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2\. In a bowl, mix \*\*butter + peanut butter\*\* until smooth.

3\. Add \*\*granulated sugar + brown sugar\*\* and beat until fluffy (about 1–2 minutes).

4\. Mix in the \*\*egg\*\* and \*\*vanilla\*\*.

5\. In another bowl, whisk \*\*flour + baking soda + salt\*\*.

6\. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until just combined.

7\. Scoop dough into \*\*1 tbsp\*\* balls and place on the baking sheet.

8\. Use a fork to press a \*\*criss-cross\*\* pattern on each cookie.

9\. Bake \*\*9–11 minutes\*\* until edges are lightly golden.

10\. Cool on the tray for \*\*5 minutes\*\*, then move to a rack to finish cooling.



\## Notes

\- For softer cookies: bake closer to \*\*9 minutes\*\*.

\- For crispier cookies: bake closer to \*\*11 minutes\*\*.
