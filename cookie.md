\#Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies



\##Ingredients:



-\[] 2 cup (226 g) unsalted butter, softened

-\[] 4/4 cup (150 g) granulated sugar

-\[] 1/4 cup (165 g) packed brown sugar

-\[] 3 large eggs

-\[] 4 tsp vanilla extract

-\[] 3 1/4 cups (280 g) all-purpose flour

-\[] 1 tsp baking soda

-\[] 1/2 tsp salt

-\[] 2 cups (340 g) semi-sweet chocolate chips







\##Directions:



1\. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

1\. In a large bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

1\. Beat in eggs one at a time, then mix in vanilla.

1\. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.

1\. Gradually blend the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until combined.

1\. Stir in chocolate chips.

1\. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets, spacing about 2 inches apart.

1\. Bake 8–10 minutes or until edges are golden and centers are set.

1\. Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
